About Generate

Generate is built by a number of artists, creators, designers and developers.

Generate builds creative tools, experiences, and features driven by a love for videos, photos, music and interactivity.

Our mission is to help everybody unleash their own unique concepts in the tap of a button.

Driven by this vision, Generate supports the artistic process from start to finish — not only providing our community with tools, but also with the education, and inspiration necessary to create work that is new and different.

Generate has had a long history of Creative Coders, Developers and Designers that have continued to push the boundaries of the current mobile video revolution, and we want to acknowledge all of their work.

Generate was founded by Malcolm Levy. Development, Design and Creative Input from its inception till now include Omar Juarez, Rafael Puyana, Samm Z Cooper, Jesse Scott, David Schwartz, Sarah Cole-Burnett, Patrick Daggitt, Marisa Olson, Leo Stefansson, Martin Schueller, Becky Wu, Melody Wang, Ronald Ho, Omid Alemi, Kristin Tretheway and Adam Marshall. Generate has strived to be an ongoing supporter of diversity in tech in its community, team, its events and user base.
